# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Visitors who arrive at `www.loamly.net` and need a clear entry point into Loamly projects. Their context is lightweight discovery: they want to understand that Loamly is a project family and choose the active destination without reading a long marketing page.

## Product Purpose
Loamly exists here as a project hub. The page should make the primary available project, Anime, immediately reachable while reserving visible space for future Loamly projects. Success means a visitor can identify the official Loamly home, understand that more projects will live here, and reach `anime.loamly.net` in one deliberate action.

## Brand Personality
Controlled, cinematic, precise. The interface should feel like a quiet future command surface rather than a loud cyberpunk poster.

## Anti-references
Avoid busy neon overload, generic SaaS cards, fake dashboard chrome, emoji-led project tiles, animated noise that obscures reading, and placeholder links that appear active before a project exists.

## Design Principles
- Make the project directory the product: navigation should be visible in the first viewport.
- Signal restraint before spectacle: futuristic materials should support clarity.
- Treat each project as a launch point, not a content card.
- Reserve future capacity without pretending future projects are live.
- Keep the page static, fast, and easy to deploy on `www.loamly.net`.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-visible focus states, reduced-motion alternatives for animated visuals, and touch targets of at least 44px. Color is used for atmosphere, not as the only way to identify active or reserved projects.
